Autumn Season

Autumn is one of the four Earth’s seasons, that goes after summer and foreshadows winter. This season also can be called as Fall and it is about big changes in nature and environment.

Autumn means a lot of fun and outdoors activities. It brings different colors to the people’s life – yellow, red, orange, brown and more. The temperature becomes colder, days shorter. Animals starting preparation for cold months and plants stop making food, everything in the nature slowly starting to fall asleep.

Plants

Almost all trees start to change their color, when the Autumn season is coming. It is very pleasant to us to look at this
amazing gold, red, yellow and others. So go outdoors and enjoy this amazing time! Feel autumn vibes everywhere.

As the weather becomes colder, the temperature of ground is colder too, that is why plants stop giving food.

The best flower to plant in Autumn is crocus. This small, but very beautiful flower will please you every day with
bright colors.
As you know, Autumn is a harvest period. A lot of farmers start to collect their crops, different fruits and vegetables.
But it is also a time when you can grow something. For example, a baby spinach, snow peas, radishes, garlic and
kale.

Animals

When the weather starting to get colder, animals, that living outside, beginning the preparation for cold seasons.
There are some ways how animals getting ready for winter.

Some animals grow warm coats! Same as we buy for us warm clothes. Rabbits grow thicker fur and that is why they
are able to survive in winter. Even cats or dogs do this, just pay attention if you have a domestic pet.

Other animals fall into special deep sleep, called hibernation. Their body temperature becomes lower and in this way
they save energy. This lifestyle applies to bears, chipmunks and skunks.
Finding a shelter is also a good plan to survive the Winter for animals. So in Autumn, they searching for some places
inside the trees, under rocks or underground.

Birds

When the Autumn season comes, most of the birds’ spices starting to migrate to the warmer places. They fly away in
areas, where the average temperature in winter is not so cold and it will be much easier for them to find food and
place to live.
